About

Hailing from Flint, Michigan, Thomas Hatt first captured attention with his groundbreaking hyperpop track "sebii/loser" from the album antilove. Known for his fearless experimentation, he has since evolved, crafting atmospheric soundscapes that challenge the way we perceive music. His upcoming album xenogamy is comprised of a fusion of EDM, pop, hyperpop, ambient, rap, and indie rock—continuing his bold journey across the music industry.

On this website, you'll find links to Thomas Hatt's music, where you can stream his latest tracks and explore past releases. There’s also a section for Drum Kits, allowing you to see and even interact with the beats behind his songs. Additionally, you can check out insights into his creative process, including behind-the-scenes looks at how his unique soundscapes come together.
